Story

I got a new Pebble Steel and always wanted to develop for the platform. I found out about the new Pebble.js platform and used it to make an app for the /r/AppHookup subreddit that I have moderated for a while now. I learned quite a lot in the process. The app lets you view several recent posts from the subreddit.

For the curious

How it works: Pebble.js is a new SDK for making apps that are processed on the phone and used from the watch. I use the reddit API to gather data about the recent posts on /r/AppHookup, do some fancy text processing and display the results in the app.
